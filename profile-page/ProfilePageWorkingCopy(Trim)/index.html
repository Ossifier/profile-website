Aspiring web designer/developer residing in Minneapolis, Minnesota. My chief goal is to combine functionality and simplicity with a distinctive and elegant aesthetic approach to front end design. I draw inspiration from architecture, art, nature, and photography. In particular, examples of my work in the latter category can be found displayed throughout this website.

This page is very much a living document, and can be expected to grow as I expand my skillset over the long term. No templates, libraries or frameworks were employed in the devlopment of this site, and was instead built "au naturel" to formally demonstrate my own personal approach to website design.

I humbly thank you for your time in perusing this page.
